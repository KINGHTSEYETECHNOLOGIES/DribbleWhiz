Information Collected Automatically

  • Usage data - pages you visit, links you click, time spent on pages

  • Send you our daily email newsletter with games, sweepstakes picks, lucky numbers and trivia

  • Prevent fraud and maintain the security of our services

4. How We Share Your Information

We do not sell your personal information. We may share your information with:

  • Email service providers - we use third-party email delivery platforms to send our newsletter. These providers process your email address on our behalf under confidentiality agreements.

  • Advertising partners - we may use email advertising platforms (including LiveIntent/Zeta Global) that display relevant ads within our emails. These partners may use anonymized, hashed versions of your email address to serve relevant advertising. They do not receive your raw email address.

  • Legal requirements - we may disclose information when required by law, court order, or government authority.

By subscribing to ZippleSnap, you consent to receive commercial email messages from us. Every email we send will include:

  • Our physical mailing address: SmartReliefTools LLC, 16192 Coastal Highway, Lewes, DE 19958

To unsubscribe from our emails, click the "Unsubscribe" link in any email we send, or contact us at support@zipplesnap.com. We will process your request within 10 business days.

We use cookies and similar tracking technologies to:

You can control cookie settings through your browser. Disabling cookies may affect the functionality of certain features on our site. Our advertising partners, including LiveIntent/Zeta Global, may also place cookies or use pixel tracking within our emails. These are governed by their own privacy policies.

California Residents (CCPA)

If you are a California resident, you have the right to:

  • Know what personal information we collect and how it is used

To exercise these rights, contact us at support@zipplesnap.com.

European Users (GDPR)

If you are located in the European Economic Area, you have rights including access, rectification, erasure, and portability of your personal data, as well as the right to object to processing. Our legal basis for processing is your consent (which you may withdraw at any time). Contact us at support@zipplesnap.com to exercise these rights.
